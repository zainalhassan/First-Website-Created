Zain Al-Hassan

I am a 19 year old who is currently studying BSc Software Engineering at Manchester Metropolitan University.

I was born on the 9th November 1998.

The main reason why I chose this course was because I had a genuine interest in the world of technology. Previously, I had studied ICT at both GCSE and A level and I enjoyed it very much. I felt that the next logical step would be to choose a computer related course as it would suit me. Software Engineering appealed to me more than Computing as it allows you to specialise in a field and gives you a taster on what life would be like as a Software Developer.

Work

I have previously worked as a part time counterstaff at Chunky Chicken. This job has given me an insight on what professional work will be like. Throughout my time here, I have learned valuable skills that have enhanced me in many ways. I have learnt how to speak to customers in a professional manner. As well as this, the job has taught me how to perform consistently under pressure as the slightest of mistakes could lead to an order being incorrectly packed.
